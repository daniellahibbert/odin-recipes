Description

Lasagna is a beloved Italian dish made with layers of pasta,
rich tomato sauce, savory ground meat, and melted cheese.
It's the ultimate comfort food and perfect for family gatherings!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28 oz) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  • 3 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese (for topping)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large pan, cook the ground beef (or sausage)
    over medium heat until browned. Add the chopped onion and garlic and cook until softened.
  3. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste,
    basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan cheese, and parsley.
  5. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce on the bottom
    of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Layer 4 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  6. Add one-third of the ricotta mixture, one-third of the meat sauce,
    and a generous handful of shredded mozzarella. Repeat with two more layers, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and mozzarella on top.
  7. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    Then, remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  8. Let the lasagna cool for about 10 minutes
    before serving. Slice and enjoy!
