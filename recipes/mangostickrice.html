Mango Sticky Rice

Home | Lasagna | Jerk Chicken | Mango Sticky Rice

Description

This is a popular Thai dessert made with sweet sticky rice, fresh mango slices, and a creamy coconut milk sauce. It’s a simple yet delicious treat perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup glutinous rice (sticky rice)
  • 1 1/4 cups coconut milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 ripe mangoes, peeled and sliced

Steps

  1. Rinse the sticky rice thoroughly in cold water and soak it in water for about 1 hour.
  2. Drain the rice and steam it for 20–30 minutes, or until soft and fully cooked.
  3. In a saucepan, combine coconut milk, sugar, and salt. Heat over low heat until the sugar dissolves (do not boil).
  4. Once the rice is cooked, mix it with the coconut milk mixture and let it sit for about 15 minutes to absorb the flavor.
  5. Serve the sticky rice warm with fresh mango slices on top or on the side. Optionally, garnish with sesame seeds or mung beans.
